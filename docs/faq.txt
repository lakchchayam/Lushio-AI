# Lushio Electronics - Frequently Asked Questions & Store Policies

## Shipping Policy
We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the continental US. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping (1-2 business days) is available for a flat rate of $15. International shipping rates vary by location and generally take 7-14 business days. All orders placed before 2 PM EST are processed on the same day.

## Return & Refund Policy
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. You can return any unopened and unused items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Opened electronics (like laptops, smartphones) may be subject to a 15% restocking fee unless they are defective. To initiate a return, please visit our online portal or contact customer support for a prepaid shipping label. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected. Software and downloadable content are strictly non-refundable.

## Warranty Information
All our electronics come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects. We do not cover accidental damage, liquid damage, or unauthorized modifications. For an additional fee, you can purchase the "Lushio Care" extended warranty, which covers accidental damage and extends the coverage to 3 years.

## Store Hours & Locations
Our customer support team is available via chat and phone Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM EST, and weekends from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST. We currently operate exclusively online and do not have any physical retail locations.

## Payment Methods
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer financing options through Affirm for orders exceeding $300.
